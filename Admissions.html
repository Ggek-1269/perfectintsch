ADMISSIONS

At PERFECT INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL, we welcome students from diverse backgrounds who are eager to embark on an educational journey filled with growth and discovery. Our admissions process is designed to be comprehensive yet straightforward, ensuring that we find the right fit for both the student and our school community.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Previous academic records
  • Birth certificate or proof of age
  • Medical records and immunization history
  • Recent passport-sized photographs

Application Process

  • Submit the completed application form along with the required documents.
  • Attend an interview with our admissions team.
  • Take an entrance examination (if applicable).
  • Receive an admission decision.
  • Complete the enrollment process and pay the necessary fees.
